Our history...

We were born in 1990 in California, it was a perfect time, lots of trouble to find and a crew ready for it! We started out in an old garaje, creating our own boards and figuring out things as we evolved into a clothing company. Nowadays we rather sell boards than ride them, but that's just what time does to people. Anyway, we decided to stop vandalizing empty house pools for fun and started thinking that there was a huge oportunity there, just waiting to be taken.
